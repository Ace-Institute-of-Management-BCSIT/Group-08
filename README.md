# Group-08

1. Study app for students and special learners: 
Currently, most students and special learners face problems like procastination, small attention span, assignment overload, and so on which leads to a negative impact in their grades and their overall learning . This project aims to make learning more interesting and interactive using various methods like pomodoro, flash cards, quizzes, etc.

2. Wardrobe Savior for teenagers: 
Currently, most teenagers face the problem of a messy closet and choosing a suitable outfit for the day which leads to loss of time, energy and confidence. This project aims to make the task of choosing outfits less confusing and fast and also allows the user to experiment with their wardrobe without making a mess.

3. Find Me app for various household works: 
Currently most households face the problem of not finding appropiate people for multiple tasks around the house such as cleaning, caretaking, plumbling, etc. which leads to loss of time and money, small pool of choices, and so on. This project aims to bring together such individuals and provide them with a platform to showcase their services making it easier for the households to better find and choose from those services as per required.
